- You switch allegiances in battle. 
- When you are [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Bewitched\|Bewitched]], you fight on behalf of the entity that bewitched you. Their allies are your allies, and your previous allegiances are suppressed.
- When [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Bewitched\|Bewitched]] you switch sides on the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Initiative\|Initiative]]. The [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Director\|Director]] indicates when the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Bewitched\|Bewitched]] creature takes their turn.
- When you take any damage during a round, at the start of the new round you make a DC 13 Flat check, on a success the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Bewitched\|Bewitched]] condition ends.
- Taking damage multiple times reduces the DC by 1 for every instance of damage.
