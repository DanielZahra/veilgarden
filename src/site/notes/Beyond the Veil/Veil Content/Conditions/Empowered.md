- When measuring [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Critical Effect\|Critical Effect]], you increase your [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/degree of success\|degree of success]] on an 8 rather than a 10 above or if you roll an 18, 19, or 20.
- Effects or traits that reduce critical threshold do no stack with [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Empowered\|Empowered]].
- When the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Empowered\|Empowered]] condition is gained from a mastery ability, but used for a skill check, you are expected to define how it is used.
    - e.g. You can use [[Empowering Strike\|Empowering Strike]] as an [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Intimidation\|Intimidation]] Social skill check by slamming your weapons in the ground shattering it.
