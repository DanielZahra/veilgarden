> You fall or are force moved into a direction.

Falling Damage:

- For every 2 spaces you are [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Falling\|Falling]] or [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Force Move\|Force Move]], you take 1 [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Crushing/Crushing Damage/Crushing Damage\|Crushing Damage]].
- If you take any damage from [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Falling\|Falling]] you gain the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Prone\|Prone]] condition. (this does not apply for[[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Force Move\|Force Move]])

---

When falling you cannot aim where you land. If you land or are [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Force Move\|Force Move]]d on a creature, that creature must attempt a DC 10 [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Skill Check/Agility Skill Check\|Agility Skill Check]]. 

Success: The creature takes no damage, and in the case of a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Force Move\|Force Move]] move, you keep moving.
Failure: The creature takes [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Crushing/Crushing Damage/Crushing Damage\|Crushing Damage]] equal to half the falling distance or [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Force Move\|Force Move]] damage you took.
Critical Failure: The creature takes the same amount of [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Crushing/Crushing Damage/Crushing Damage\|Crushing Damage]] you took from the fall or [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Force Move\|Force Move]].

---

When [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Falling\|Falling]] or being [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Force Move\|Force Move]] you or another creature can use the [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Universal  Actions/Grab On\|Grab On]] reaction to stop you from moving.
