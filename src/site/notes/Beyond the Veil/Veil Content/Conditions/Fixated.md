- You are fixed on a singular enemy.
- While you are aware of the Fixator, you take a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Bane/Major Bane\|Major Bane]] to Prowess on any **offensive** action that **doesn’t include the Fixator** as a target or one of the targets. 
- This penalty doesn’t apply to movement, purely defensive actions, or actions that include the Fixator.
- You can only be [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Fixated\|Fixated]] by one creature at a time, the last [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Fixated\|Fixated]] applied is used.
- If you become unaware of the Fixator or they are [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Hidden\|Hidden]] from you, they are [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Unconscious\|Unconscious]] or dead, [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Fixated\|Fixated]] ends.
