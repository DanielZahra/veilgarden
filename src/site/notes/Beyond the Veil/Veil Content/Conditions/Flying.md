- To be [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Flying\|Flying]] you must have [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Speed/Flying Speed\|Flying Speed]]. You can use the [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Universal  Actions/Fly\|Fly]] to [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Universal  Actions/Move\|Move]] 0 distance to [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Hover\|Hover]] in place.
- If you're airborne at the end of your turn and didn't use a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Movement\|Movement]] action this round, you fall.
