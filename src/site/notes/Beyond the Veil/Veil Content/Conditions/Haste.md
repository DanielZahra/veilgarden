- When you gain [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Haste\|Haste]] you can trigger it as a free action and gain one additional action. This ends [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Haste\|Haste]]. 
- Unless specified [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Haste\|Haste]] ends at the end of the encounter. 
- You can use one instance of [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Haste\|Haste]] once per round.
- A [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Haste\|Haste]] can give you multiple charges of haste, which allow you to use it multiple times on different rounds. Example [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Haste\|Haste]] 2, allows you to use haste twice in 2 different rounds.
