> You gain supernatural smell, hearing, sight and taste. 

- All[[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Skill Check/Perception Skill Check\|Perception Skill Check]] are made with a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Boon/Major Boon\|Major Boon]]
- Creatures that are [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Concealed\|Concealed]] to you are now normally visible to you.
- Creatures that are [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Veiled\|Veiled]] to you are now [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Concealed\|Concealed]] to you.
- Creatures that are [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Invisible-Unseen\|Invisible-Unseen]] to you are now [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Veiled\|Veiled]] to you.
- [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Heightened Senses\|Heightened Senses]] is always followed by a number which indicates the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Range/Range\|Range]] where it is effective.
