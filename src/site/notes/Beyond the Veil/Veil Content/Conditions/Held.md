- While being [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Held\|Held]] but another entity, your [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Speed/Speed\|Speed]] is 0.
- In cases where the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Held\|Held]] condition’s source is a [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Universal  Actions/Grapple\|Grapple]], the [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Universal  Actions/Grapple\|Grapple]] and [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Held\|Held]] are removed if the creatures are not longer in contact.
- You can use the [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Universal  Actions/Escape\|Escape]] action to free yourself from being [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Held\|Held]].
