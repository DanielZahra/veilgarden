- While you have [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Hover\|Hover]] you can float off the ground. Effect that require you to be on the ground have no effect on you.
- Unless indicated otherwise, [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Hover\|Hover]] allows you to float roughly 30 cm or 1 foot off the ground.
