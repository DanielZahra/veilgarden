- You are [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Hidden\|Hidden]] in plain sight.
- You are completely invisible to the naked eye.  And can only be made visible if your invisibility is removed or a creature uses other senses or detection methods, other than sight.
- You gain a[[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Boon/Major Boon\|Major Boon]] to [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Universal  Actions/Hide\|Hide]] Action and [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Skills/Subterfuge\|Subterfuge]] Skill Checks.
- If you [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Attack\|Attack]] a creature while you are [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Hidden\|Hidden]] or [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Invisible-Unseen\|Invisible-Unseen]] the creature is [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Exposed\|Exposed]] to you.
