- When you have [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Paralysis\|Paralysis]], any action you spend needs to pass a DC 5 [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Flat Check\|Flat Check]]. On Failure, you whatever you were doing is stopped.
- On success [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Paralysis\|Paralysis]] has no effect on the action.
