- When you have the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Swift\|Swift]] condition, other creatures cannot use reactions against you.
- While you have [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Swift\|Swift]] you can move through other creature’s spaces but cannot stop in them.
