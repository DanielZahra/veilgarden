- You are [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Prone\|Prone]] and cannot take any actions and skip your turn.
- An adjacent creature can use an [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Universal  Actions/Interact\|Interact]] action to wake you up.
- All attacks against you are done with [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Advantage\|Advantage]]
- If you take damage [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Unconscious\|Unconscious]] ends, but you would still be [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Prone\|Prone]].
- Any damage you take while [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Unconscious\|Unconscious]] gives you a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Reaper’s Mark\|Reaper’s Mark]].
- [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Unconscious\|Unconscious]] lasts 1d6 hours unless specified.
