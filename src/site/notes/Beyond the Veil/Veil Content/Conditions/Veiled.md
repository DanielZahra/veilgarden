> When you are [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Veiled\|Veiled]] you and any objects you are carrying bend the light around you and make you translucent making it incredibly difficult to see you. 

- Any attacks or abilities use directly against you, need to pass a DC 11 Flat check or they automatically critically fail.
- You can use [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Universal  Actions/Hide\|Hide]] and [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Mastery Actions/Sneak\|Sneak]] without being in Cover.
- You gain a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Boon/Boon\|Boon]] to [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Universal  Actions/Hide\|Hide]] Action and [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Skills/Subterfuge\|Subterfuge]] Skill Checks.
- [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Veiled\|Veiled]] is removed if you take [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Direct Damage\|Direct Damage]] from a successful [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Attack\|Attack]] or Ability that targets you.
