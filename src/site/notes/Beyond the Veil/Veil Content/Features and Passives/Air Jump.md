---
Description:: Once per round after a [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Universal  Actions/Jump/Jump\|Jump]]  you can perform a second [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Universal  Actions/Jump/Jump\|Jump]] mid air as a free action, as if the air itself was ground or a wall. When rolling for the jump, you still make a single roll, but cover the distance as if you jumped twice.
