You can hold a weapon with a 1+ trait in one hand, but the process of using it requires using a second hand to operate any ammunition or mechanism that is required to [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Strike\|Strike]]. This means you can do things with your free hand while holding the 1+ Hand weapon without changing your grip, but the other hand must be free when you use the weapon. To properly wield a 1+ weapon, you must hold it in one hand and also have a hand free.
