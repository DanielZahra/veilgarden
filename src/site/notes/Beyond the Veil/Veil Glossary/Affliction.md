Affiliations are physical wounds, maladies or injuries and don’t simply go away after a good night’s sleep. A [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Bane\|Bane]] applied by an [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Affliction\|Affliction]] is permanent until you remove the affliction.
