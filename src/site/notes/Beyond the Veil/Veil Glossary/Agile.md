>This weapon is light, easy to wield and becomes more dangerous as you attack and build momentum

For every **successful** [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Strike\|Strike]] on a turn with an [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Agile\|Agile]] weapon, your next [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Strike\|Strike]] gains 1 [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/additional damage\|additional damage]]. [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Agile\|Agile]] has a limit of 3 [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/additional damage\|additional damage]]. e.g. One successful strike, next strike deals 1 [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/additional damage\|additional damage]]. Two successful strikes, next strike deals 2 [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/additional damage\|additional damage]]. [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/One Handed\|One Handed]] [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Agile\|Agile]] weapons are required for [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Dual Wielding\|Dual Wielding]].
