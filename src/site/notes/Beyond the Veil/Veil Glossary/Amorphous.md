Ooze, jellies, morphing, mimic Creatures whose physical forms lack fixed structure. Their bodies are composed of fluid, gel, mist, shadow, or shifting organic matter that can deform at will. Amorphous beings can squeeze through tight spaces, cling to surfaces, and reshape themselves instinctively. They may be natural, magical, elemental, void-touched, or created through alchemy or corruption.
