[[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Anima/Anima\|Anima]] targets the mind, the soul, the spirit and your psyche. This often psychic, divine or strange energies form the cosmos.  Anima useful for dealing with metaphysical and incorporeal entities. [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Anima/Anima Damage\|Anima Damage]] lets you harm a ghost possessing a person without harming the person itself. [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Anima/Anima\|Anima]] while being a trait is also a damage type. [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Anima/Anima Damage\|Anima Damage]], typically has no effect on mindless or soulless creatures
