### [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Veil Roll\|Veil Roll]] 

When you have a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Bane/Bane\|Bane]]  to your attributes, skills, or against a condition or effect, you simple add a -2 or -4 to any roll made.

### Damage 

When you have a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Bane/Bane\|Bane]] against a specific damage type, whenever you take said damage you take 2 or 4 more.
