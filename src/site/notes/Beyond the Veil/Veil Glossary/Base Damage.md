When the wording [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Base Damage\|Base Damage]] is used, it refers to damage done which is the innate or original damage of the ability or weapon. Any [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/additional damage\|additional damage]], [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Imbue\|Imbue]], [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Manafused/Manafused\|Manafused]] or [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Attuned/Attuned\|Attuned]] is not part of the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Base Damage\|Base Damage]]. When a feature applies multiple effects based on a type [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Base Damage\|Base Damage]] and there are multiples, you simply choose one of the available.
