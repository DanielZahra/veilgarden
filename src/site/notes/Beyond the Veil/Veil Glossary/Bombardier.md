When you use the [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Universal  Actions/Reload Action\|Reload Action]] with a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Bombardier\|Bombardier]] weapon, you can choose to load any [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Bomb\|Bomb]] from your [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Loadout\|Loadout]] to be fired on your next shot. The next [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Universal  Actions/Strike Action/Strike Action\|Strike Action]] you take will shoot the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Bomb\|Bomb]]. If you make any other type of [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Attack\|Attack]] with this weapon, the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Bomb\|Bomb]] is used up and cannot be reused, and you can make another [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Attack\|Attack]] normally. When you use the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Bombardier\|Bombardier]] trait, you can shoot any [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Bomb\|Bomb]] replacing the bomb’s [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Thrown/Thrown\|Thrown]] trait with the weapon’s [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Range/Range\|Range]] trait. Any [Attack](https://app.notion.com/p/Attack-1dc75a22781a80d48afbf73509313634?pvs=21) done this way uses the traits of the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Bomb\|Bomb]] not those of the weapon (except for the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Range/Range\|Range]]). If your [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Bombardier\|Bombardier]]weapon has no [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Reload/Reload\|Reload]], you can simply load or shoot the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Bomb\|Bomb]] normally.
