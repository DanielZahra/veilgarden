[[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Veil Roll\|Veil Roll]]: When you have a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Boon\|Boon]] to your attributes, skills, or against a condition or effect, you simple add a +2 to any roll made.

Damage Type: When you have a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Boon\|Boon]] against a specific damage type, whenever you take said damage you take 2 less damage.
