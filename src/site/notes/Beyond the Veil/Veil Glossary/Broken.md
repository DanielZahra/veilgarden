Broken is used for [[Gear and Consumables\|Gear and Consumables]]. A broken item cannot be used and also cannot be repaired with [[Field Repair\|Field Repair]]. This item is restored after a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Rest/Rest\|Rest]]. 
Broken [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Weapons/Weapons\|Weapons]] or [[Protection\|Protection]] receive a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Bane/Major Bane\|Major Bane]] to Defense or Prowess until you Rest.
