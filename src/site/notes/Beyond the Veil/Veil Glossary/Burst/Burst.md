- A burst is area that represents a square of equal dimensions from both sides. 
- When placing a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Burst/Burst\|Burst]] you can 'anchor' it from anywhere within your reach.
- e.g. 2x2, 3x3, etc.
