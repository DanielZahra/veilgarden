> [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Cargo\|Cargo]] is an additional storage that you hold in your camp.

Every upgrade the party buys in [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Campcraft\|Campcraft]] takes 1 [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Cargo\|Cargo]]. Excess [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Wealth\|Wealth]] can be stored in the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Cargo\|Cargo]] space. Items that are bought as a stack take only 1 [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Cargo\|Cargo]] slot. Items in the Cargo can be accessed during rest and downtime. Cargo Starts at 20 Capacity

### Exceptions

- Rituals do not take [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Cargo\|Cargo]] space.
