When you [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Attack\|Attack]] a target, you can target an additional creature within your reach. The creature takes half of the weapon damage on a success; success is calculated on the main target of the attack. No other effects are applied. [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Cleave\|Cleave]] can only be applied to an [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Attack\|Attack]] that targets a single creature.
