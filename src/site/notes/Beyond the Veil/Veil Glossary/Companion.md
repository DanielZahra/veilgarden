> Companions are either, Familiars, Pets, Summons, Mounts etc. Abilities with this trait usually can only be used by or on a Companion.
- in [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Exploration Mode\|Exploration Mode]] when a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Companion\|Companion]] is not given specific orders, it typically follows its master.
- Abilities with the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Companion\|Companion]] trait can be activated during a Companion’s turn and only when the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Companion\|Companion]] is active.

# Companions Rules

- [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Companion\|Companion]]s are always the same level as the owner.
- When you gain a new level of mastery for your [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Companion\|Companion]] you can ‘rebuild’ the companion during the same downtime.
- Effects that trigger at the start of the turn or end of the turn for the companion trigger on the** your** start or end of turn.
- Abilities with the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Companion\|Companion]] Trait can be used by either you or your [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Companion\|Companion]].
- For a companion to use a Reaction, the master must use their own reaction.
- [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Companion\|Companion]]s have no [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Loadout\|Loadout]] of their own, unless they have traits which give them [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Loadout\|Loadout]]. 
    - Runes, Weapons, and [[Soulbound\|Soulbound]] items are all placed and use the owner’s Loadout or bind slots.
