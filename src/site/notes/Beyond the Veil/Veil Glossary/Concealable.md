> This weapon is designed to be inconspicuous, does not look like a weapon or easily concealed. 

When this weapon is involved in a check or DC to conceal it, the result is always has its [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/degree of success\|degree of success]] increased by 1 in favor of you hiding it, or concealing its identity as a weapon.
