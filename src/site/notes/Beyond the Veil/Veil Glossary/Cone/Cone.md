- Cones are area abilities or attacks that affect a 90 degree cone.
- [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Cone/Cone\|Cone]] is always followed by a number which indicates the length of ‘number’ of cone layers.
    - e.g. a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Cone/Cone 3\|Cone 3]]. would start from 1 -2 -3 layers.
