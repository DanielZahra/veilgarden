[[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Consumable\|Consumable]] items have a finite use. Typically each uses consumes the item. A large part of [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Consumable\|Consumable]] items bought from [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Campcraft\|Campcraft]] are the recipes or blueprints for you to stock up on the item with each [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Rest/Rest\|Rest]]. Some items such as [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Gear/Ability Stone (Adept)\|Ability Stone (Adept)]] cannot be bought and only found as loot or rewards. Once used, these items are not restocked.
