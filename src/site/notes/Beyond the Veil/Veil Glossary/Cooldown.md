- Normal Cooldown: Any Ability or Item with a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Cooldown\|Cooldown]] means it cannot be activated again until you [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Exploration Activities/Take a Break\|Take a Break]]
- Once Per Rest: You need to [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Rest/Rest\|Rest]] to reuse this item.
- Round: This means the items or ability can be used once per round.

When any action with a cooldown is interrupted or is stopped from being used, the cooldown is still activated.
