This statement infers that a creatures that is in contact or moves through an area is effected by whatever ability, feature or effect is present. When this effect is set the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Prowess\|Prowess]] roll that created it is static, based on when the effect was created. These effects can only trigger once per round, per creature.
