When critical damage is dealt you simply double the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Base Damage\|Base Damage]] with a strike or ability. Any [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/additional damage\|additional damage]] that applies to the main effect is not doubled unless specifically indicated. Some features, traits or abilities might apply special effects on conditions when you roll a critical success.
