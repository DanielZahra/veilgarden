Damaged is usually used for [[Gear and Consumables\|Gear and Consumables]]. When an item is damaged it cannot be used but it can be repaired using [[Field Repair\|Field Repair]]. Damaged [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Weapons/Weapons\|Weapons]] or [[Protection\|Protection]] receive a Bane to Defense or Prowess until you Rest.
