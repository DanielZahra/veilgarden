The [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Device\|Device]] trait signifies an object that can be used. Devices usually have a specific use or trigger. Devices include. Typically devices are [[Manatech\|Manatech]] or Mechanical, and they have a number of uses after which they need to be recharged or reset during Rest. A [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Device\|Device]] needs to be in your [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Loadout\|Loadout]] or your are holding it to be used.
