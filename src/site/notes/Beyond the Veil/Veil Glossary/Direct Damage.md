[[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Direct Damage\|Direct Damage]] is, damage that you deal immediately when you are using an ability, [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Strike\|Strike]] or effect. Any damage that is applied on a creature’s turn or caused by the creature doing something, or the environment is considered [[indirect damage\|indirect damage]].
