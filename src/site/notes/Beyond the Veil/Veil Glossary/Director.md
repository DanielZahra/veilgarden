The [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Director\|Director]] sometimes also referred to as the Game Master, controls the world, NPCs and also serves as a referee for the rules. The [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Director\|Director]] is also often responsible for rolling secret checks for the players.
