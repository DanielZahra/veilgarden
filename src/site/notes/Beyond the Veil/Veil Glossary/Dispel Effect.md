> When you dispel an effect, you can suppress it momentarily or eliminate it permanently. Dispelling allows you to bend the rules of nature and reality for some time as you block the course through your own will and power.

- The DC used is based on the creature that created the effect.
- If the effect is created using an item, the creature that triggered the item uses its own DC.

---

[[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Dispel Effect\|Dispel Effect]] has no effect on:

- Effects that have a permanent duration: such as Curses, Afflictions, and Magical Gear
    - This also applies to effects that essentially have no duration because they are part of the world. (e.g., you cannot dispel a natural waterfall or [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Cover/Cover\|Cover]] created by the environment).
- Abilities with the traits: [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Transformation\|Transformation]],  [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Stance\|Stance]]. 
- Conditions: [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Held\|Held]], [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Prone\|Prone]], [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Unconscious\|Unconscious]] or [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Hidden\|Hidden]]
- TBD (Some more cases)

---

To dispel an effect, you make a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Prowess\|Prowess]] [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Veil Roll\|Veil Roll]] against the DC of the effect. If you believe or know that a target has more than one effect, you choose a single one.
**Critical Success**: You dispel the effect completely. 
**Success**: You dispel the effect for 10 minutes. 
**Failure**: No Effect.
