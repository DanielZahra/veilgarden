[[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Dual Wielding\|Dual Wielding]] is the act of holding a weapon in each hand. Abilities or features that call the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Dual Wielding\|Dual Wielding]] trait allow you to use both weapons at the same time for the purposes of damage. Only [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Agile\|Agile]] weapons can be used in [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Dual Wielding\|Dual Wielding]]. When you make a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Dual Wielding\|Dual Wielding]] attack, you make 1 [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Prowess\|Prowess]] [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Veil Roll\|Veil Roll]] and this counts for both weapons. When calculating traits and damage, each weapon’s output is resolved separately, then the final results are added for purposes of Damage [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Boon/Boon\|Boon]]s or [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Bane/Bane\|Bane]]s. Traits of the same type are not cumulative. (TBD for mechanical Explanation, the goal is that Dual Wielding is not too powerful where Traits get stacked. Stuff like [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Deadly/Deadly\|Deadly]] cannot be applied twice , critical effects and imbue effects are only applied once)
