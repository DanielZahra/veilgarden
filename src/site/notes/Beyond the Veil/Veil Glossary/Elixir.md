Elixirs are small vials, potions or pouched filled with a mixture (usually liquid) that is beneficial for those imbibing it. Elixirs require an [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Actions/Interact\|Interact]] Action to be used from your [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Loadout\|Loadout]]. When applying an elixir to another creature you need to be within [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Adjacent\|Adjacent]]range.
