When you use an effect that includes an [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Emanation/Emanation\|Emanation]] you can decide to include yourself or not. Emanations extend from your space into adjacent spaces. If you are of a larger size, an emanation is also bigger. Emanation is the total ‘reach’ away from your spot. [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Emanation/Emanation\|Emanation]] includes a number which denotes the size of the reach of the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Emanation/Emanation\|Emanation]].
