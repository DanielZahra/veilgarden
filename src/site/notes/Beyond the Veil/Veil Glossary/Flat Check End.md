When a duration lasts until [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Flat Check End\|Flat Check End]], the duration is variable. For any conditions or effect you are suffering from that lasts until [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Flat Check End\|Flat Check End]], at the end of your turn you make a DC 11 [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Flat Check\|Flat Check]].
