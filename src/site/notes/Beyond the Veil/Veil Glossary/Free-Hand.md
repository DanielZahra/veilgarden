This weapon doesn’t take up your hand. You can use the hand covered by your free-hand weapon to wield other items, perform [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Universal  Actions/Interact\|Interact]] actions, and so on. You can’t attack with a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Free-Hand\|Free-Hand]] weapon if you’re wielding/holding anything in that hand or otherwise using that hand. Each of your hands can have only one [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Free-Hand\|Free-Hand]] weapon on it.
