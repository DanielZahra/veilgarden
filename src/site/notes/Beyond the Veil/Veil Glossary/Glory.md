[[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Glory\|Glory]] abilities are  special abilities that can be used once per rest. When an ability has a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Glory\|Glory]] action cost, it means both participants pay the action cost of the ability. If the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Glory\|Glory]] ability impacts another ability use, the cost of the action is part of the glory ability itself.
