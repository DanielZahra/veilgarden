A [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Glyph\|Glyph]] is a magical symbol that has to be placed on a stationary object, a floor, the ground, etc. When the Glyph is created, it uses special material to leave a mark or design. If the design is damaged, the Glyph loses it’s power.
