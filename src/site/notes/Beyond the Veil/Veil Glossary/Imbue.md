Imbue effects add an additional effect or damage to your [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Strike\|Strike]]. This includes your unarmed strikes. When you have multiple [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Imbue\|Imbue]] effects active, you can choose which one is used when you use any actions.
