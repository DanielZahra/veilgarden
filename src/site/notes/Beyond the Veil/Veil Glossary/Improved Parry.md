> This weapon can be used defensively to block attacks. While wielding this weapon, you use a reaction to turn a miss.

Requirement: You are wielding a weapon with the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Improved Parry\|Improved Parry]] Trait.

---

**Trigger** A creature makes an attack against your [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Defense\|Defense]] that hits by matching your [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Defense\|Defense]] or by a **1 to 3 difference**. Alternatively, you can turn a failed attack into a critical failure.

---

You use your weapon to deflect the attack, turning the hit into a miss or a failure in the case of a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Power Attack\|Power Attack]].
