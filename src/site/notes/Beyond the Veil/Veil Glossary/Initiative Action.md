### [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Initiative Action\|Initiative Action]] 

When an ability of a feature has the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Initiative Action\|Initiative Action]], it allows it to be used as a [[Reaction\|Reaction]] just as initiative starts. You have 1 [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Initiative Action\|Initiative Action]], if you gain multiple [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Initiative Action\|Initiative Action]]s, they still cost one reaction to use.
