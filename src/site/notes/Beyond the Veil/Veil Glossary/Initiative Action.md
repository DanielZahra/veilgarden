When you enter [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Encounter Mode\|Encounter Mode]], you can use your first turn's [[Reaction\|Reaction]] to use an action as an [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Initiative Action\|Initiative Action]]. The [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Initiative Action\|Initiative Action]] can be any single action that does not involve a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Prowess\|Prowess]] roll, [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Attack\|Attack]] or is a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Movement\|Movement]] action. Any [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Initiative Action\|Initiative Action]] you use is part of your first turn, but costs you your [[Reaction\|Reaction]] not actions. You have 1 [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Initiative Action\|Initiative Action]], if you gain multiple [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Initiative Action\|Initiative Action]]s, they still cost one [[Reaction\|Reaction]] to use. You do not gain any [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Initiative Action\|Initiative Action]]s if you are [[Ambushed\|Ambushed]].
