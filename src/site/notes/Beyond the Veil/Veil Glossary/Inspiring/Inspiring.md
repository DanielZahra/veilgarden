- Abilities with the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Inspiring/Inspiring\|Inspiring]] trait can be modified through a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Weapons/Banner\|Banner]] weapon.
{ #bf6fca}

- The [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Inspiring/Inspiring\|Inspiring]] weapon trait increases the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Emanation/Emanation\|Emanation]] or [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Range/Range\|Range]] of [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Inspiring/Inspiring\|Inspiring]] abilities by the number following the trait. Such as [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Inspiring/Inspiring 1\|Inspiring 1]] increases [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Emanation/Emanation\|Emanation]] or [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Range/Range\|Range]] by 1.
