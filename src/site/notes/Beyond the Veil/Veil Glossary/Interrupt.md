The [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Interrupt\|Interrupt]] trait allows an ability, feature to attack to stop another actions dead in it’s tracks. When something suffers from an [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Interrupt\|Interrupt]], if the action has limited usage or a cooldown, the cooldown is still activated or usage wasted.
