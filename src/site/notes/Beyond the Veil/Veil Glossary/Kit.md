> Kits are sets of equipment that allow you to perform specific jobs. They are necessary for picking locks, disarming traps, performing rituals, treating infections, etc.

To use a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Kit\|Kit]] you need to hold it or have it in your [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Loadout\|Loadout]].
