[[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Loadout\|Loadout]] represents, the inventory slots that you have to quickly access to. You start with 6 slots. Items take 1 loadout slot per item. Items with the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Loadout\|Loadout]] trait take a slot in your loadout, and need to be in your [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Loadout\|Loadout]] to be used.
