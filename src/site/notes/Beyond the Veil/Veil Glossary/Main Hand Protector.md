> You use your shield to cover your ranged abilities.

When using [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Universal  Actions/Guard\|Guard]], and you are using a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Range/Range\|Range]] weapon or ability, adjacent creatures cannot trigger reaction attacks to interrupt your ranged attacks.
