[[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Veil Roll\|Veil Roll]]: When you have a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Major Bane\|Major Bane]] to your attributes, skills, or against a condition or effect, you simple add a -4 to any roll made.

Damage Type: When you have a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Major Bane\|Major Bane]] against a specific damage type, whenever you take said damage you take 4 additional damage.
