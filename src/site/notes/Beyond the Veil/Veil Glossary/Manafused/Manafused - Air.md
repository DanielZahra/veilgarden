When you gain the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Manafused/Manafused\|Manafused]] Trait, you set a single damage type as the trait. (this can be changed with the [[Weapon and Armor Maintenance\|Weapon and Armor Maintenance]] downtime activity. If you gain a specific [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Manafused/Manafused\|Manafused]] trait such as [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Manafused/Manafused - Fire\|Manafused - Fire]], this cannot be switched. [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Manafused/Manafused\|Manafused]] weapon increases the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Base Damage\|Base Damage]] of an ability of a specific damage type by 1. [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Manafused/Manafused\|Manafused]] is not applied to [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Persistent/Persistent Damage\|Persistent Damage]] or [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Strike\|Strike]] Damage. Only one [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Manafused/Manafused\|Manafused]] effect can be applied to an ability use.
