[[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Misfire\|Misfire]] weapon gets jammed when you roll a 1 [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Veil Roll\|Veil Roll]] when using a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Strike\|Strike]].  Once jammed, the weapon needs 2 [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Universal  Actions/Reload Action\|Reload Action]]s to be unjammed. When unjammed the weapon is loaded and ready to be fired.
