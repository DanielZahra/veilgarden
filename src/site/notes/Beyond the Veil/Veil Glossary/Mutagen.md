> Mutagen are special concoctions that are powerful and mutate the creature that consumes them. This mutation is temporary and often gives a large degree of power but also has side effects

You can only have one activate [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Mutagen\|Mutagen]] at a time. When a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Mutagen\|Mutagen]] wears off you gain [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Exhaustion\|Exhaustion]] 1 until you [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Rest/Rest\|Rest]].
