Attacks with this weapon are [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Non-lethal\|Non-lethal]] and are used to knock creatures unconscious instead of kill them.  When using [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Non-lethal\|Non-lethal]] weapons, your can make [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Non-lethal\|Non-lethal]] attacks without modifying your degree of success.
