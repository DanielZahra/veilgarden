[[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Object Vitality\|Object Vitality]] is the durability of an object. Objects do not receive [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Reaper’s Mark\|Reaper’s Mark]], and are [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Damaged\|Damaged]] or [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Broken\|Broken]] when their [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Vitality\|Vitality]] is 0.  Objects cannot be critical hit nor do they suffer from critical effects.
