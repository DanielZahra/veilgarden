Oils are magical refined liquids that are used to empower weapons, armor or other objects with special properties. Oils require an [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Actions/Interact\|Interact]] action to be used from your [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Loadout\|Loadout]]. When applying an Oil to another creature you need to be within [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Adjacent\|Adjacent]] range.
