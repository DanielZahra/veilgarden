While using a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/One-Hand Reload\|One-Hand Reload]] you can use the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Actions/Reload Action\|Reload Action]] with the same hand that the weapon is being held, meaning you don’t need a free hand to reload this weapon.
