- When [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Overpower/Overpower\|Overpower]] is present the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Attack\|Attack]] targets the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Defense\|Defense]] normally, however the ability or feature will also target a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Fortitude\|Fortitude]], [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Reflex\|Reflex]] or [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Will\|Will]]. When this occurs, the result of the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Veil Roll\|Veil Roll]] made for the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Attack\|Attack]] is used for both checks.
- An [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Overpower/Overpower\|Overpower]] an only be triggered if any damage is dealt to [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Vitality\|Vitality]].  If the whole damage is negated by a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Barrier\|Barrier]], [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Boon\|Boon]], or [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Mastery Actions/Shield Block\|Shield Block]] or other damage reductions, [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Overpower/Overpower\|Overpower]] is not applied.
- [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Overpower/Overpower\|Overpower]]’s success or failure is calculated from the original result roll for an [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Attack\|Attack]] and is not modified by traits that change the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/degree of success\|degree of success]] for the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Attack\|Attack]]
- Example: You are striking a wolf, which has Defense of 8 and Fortitude 10. Your attack is a [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Universal  Actions/Strike Action/Power Strike\|Power Strike]] with [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Overpower/Overpower\|Overpower]] that applies a condition. You make a Veil Roll and roll a 14, this means your [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Strike\|Strike]] was successful, you deal damage and the condition is applied to the wolf since your total Veil Roll + Prowess was higher than the wolf’s [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Fortitude\|Fortitude]]
