When you [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Attack\|Attack]] a target, you can also hit **1** additional creature in the same line. (directly behind your target). The creature takes half of the weapon damage on a success; success is calculated on the main target of the attack. No other effects are applied. [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Penetrating\|Penetrating]] can only be applied to an [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Attack\|Attack]] that targets a single creature.
