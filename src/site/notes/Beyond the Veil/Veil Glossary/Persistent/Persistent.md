[[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Persistent/Persistent Damage\|Persistent Damage]]

- Persistent Damage, is damage of any kind that is applied at the start of each turn. [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Persistent/Persistent Damage\|Persistent Damage]] lasts until the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/End of Encounter\|End of Encounter]].
- When [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Persistent/Persistent Damage\|Persistent Damage]] is applied without a specific type, it is the same type of the base damage of the ability or weapon.
