Abilities or Items may call for a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Power Attack\|Power Attack]]. You make an [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Attack\|Attack]].

---
**Critical Success**: You deal double [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Base Damage\|Base Damage]].
**Success**: You deal normal [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Base Damage\|Base Damage]].
**Failure**: You deal half total damage.
**Critical Failure**: You deal no damage.
