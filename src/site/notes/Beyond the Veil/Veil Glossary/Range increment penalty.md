When you attack with a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Range/Range\|Range]] or [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Thrown/Thrown\|Thrown]] from beyond your range you suffer a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Range increment penalty\|Range increment penalty]]. The Range increment is the same as the range of the weapon. So a weapon with [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Range/Range 6\|Range 6]] has increments at 7, 13, 19 etc. For the first [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Range increment penalty\|Range increment penalty]] your [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Strike\|Strike]] and all added effects are one degree of success lower. For the second [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Range increment penalty\|Range increment penalty]] your [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Strike\|Strike]] and all added effects are two degree of success lower. e.g. If you [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Strike\|Strike]] with a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Weapons/Bow\|Bow]] which has [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Range/Range 12\|Range 12]], from 14 Range, you suffer 1 [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Range increment penalty\|Range increment penalty]] so a successful attack becomes a failure.
