> A repeating weapon can shoot a number of ammunition without needing to be reloaded. It typically has a magazine with automatic loading or special self loading ammunition. When this ammunition stocks runs out, a repeating weapon needs to be reloaded normally.

- [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Repeating/Repeating\|Repeating]] weapons allow you make a number of [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Strike\|Strike]] before needing to take the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Actions/Reload Action\|Reload Action]].
- [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Repeating/Repeating\|Repeating]] is followed by a number indicating how many [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Strike\|Strike]] you can make with this weapon before needing to take the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Actions/Reload Action\|Reload Action]].
