### Quick Rest (6 hours)

- When resting, the party replenishes all consumables that they have unlocked through campcraft and also any gear that might have been broken or lost is replaced (such as lanterns/ropes, etc)
- When resting, you recover two points of [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Reaper’s Mark\|Reaper’s Mark]]
