You can only rest **once** per day (24 hours). Resting takes a 6 or 12 hours for you to set up camp, secure the perimeter, eat, drink, sleep and prepare for the next day. This time also assumes and includes you performing downtime activities. Everything that doesn’t get recovered with a Take a Break Recovers with a Rest.  If you decide to rest multiple times in a row to recover further, the above rule of one rest per day still applies so it takes 2 days to accumulate 2 rests.

### Sleeping and Travel

When you are travelling through non-dangerous places ([[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Director\|Director]]’s choice), and you need to sleep for the day, you can decide to simply sleep without triggering a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Rest/Rest\|Rest]]. This allows you to retain all preparations you have made but you also gain no benefit from a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Rest/Rest\|Rest]].

There are two types of resting; you can choose to simply recover as quickly as possible or take some extra time to take downtime.

### Quick Rest (6 hours)

- When resting, the party replenishes all consumables that they have unlocked through campcraft and also any gear that might have been broken or lost is replaced (such as lanterns/ropes, etc)
- When resting, you recover two points of [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Reaper’s Mark\|Reaper’s Mark]]
