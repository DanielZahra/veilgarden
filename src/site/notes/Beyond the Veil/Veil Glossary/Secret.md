When the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Secret\|Secret]] trait is present, the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Veil Roll\|Veil Roll]] roll result is hidden from you and only the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Director\|Director]] narrates the result. This can be done by either the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Director\|Director]] making the roll, or you make the roll, but not see the result.
