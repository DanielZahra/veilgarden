Shards are unstable trinkets, scrolls or other items with magic infused in them. When you utilize a shard to unleash magic within, you roll a 1d4. On a 1, the shard is permanently destroyed after it unleashes its magic one last time.
A shard requires one or more [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Actions/Interact\|Interact]] actions to use. If it triggers an ability, it uses the action cost of the ability as the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Actions/Interact\|Interact]] cost.
