Starting at Veteran you can start spending XP to make an ability a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Signature/Signature\|Signature]] ability, you can enhance it with new effects or change its effects to do something new. When using a Signature ability you can choose only a SINGLE signature effect, and you can also choose not to add any effect to your ability.,
