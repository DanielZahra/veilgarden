Effects with the Size Difference trait receive a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Bane/Bane\|Bane]] based on size difference. When you use an ability or action with a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Size Difference\|Size Difference]] trait, you receive a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Bane/Bane\|Bane]] for each level of size difference between you and your target. 

e.g.. If you are [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Medium\|Medium]] sized and using a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Size Difference\|Size Difference]] action such as [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Universal  Actions/Shove\|Shove]] against a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Large\|Large]] creature, you make your roll with a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Bane/Bane\|Bane]]

e.g.. If you are [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Medium\|Medium]] sized and using a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Size Difference\|Size Difference]] action such as [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Universal  Actions/Trip\|Trip]] against a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Huge\|Huge]] creature, you make your roll with a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Bane/Major Bane\|Major Bane]]. 

---

Effects that modify your [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Size Difference\|Size Difference]] difference do not stack, the larger bonus is applied.
