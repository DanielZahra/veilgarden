- Can share the same space as other creatures of the same size or smaller. (Up to 2 Small Creatures can fit in a 1x1).
- When you are 2 sizes smaller than another creature, you can move through the larger creature’s space and treat it as normal terrain, however, you cannot stop in said space. This includes non-allied creatures.
- 1kg to 50 kg
- Grid Size 1x1
- A cat, fairy or dog.
