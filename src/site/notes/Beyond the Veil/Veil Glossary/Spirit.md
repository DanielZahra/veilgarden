When you use a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Spirit\|Spirit]], if the area has an existing Spirit, you make a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Dispel Effect\|Dispel Effect]] check to cancel the other spirit. There can only be one Spirit active in an area. An area that has a permanent [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Spirit\|Spirit]] effect, it cannot be replaced.
