This weapon or ability deal main damage normally to the main target of the attack, and deals [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Splash/Splash\|Splash]] damage to adjacent target. [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Splash/Splash\|Splash]] damage hits the main target and all adjacent targets/creatures of your choice. [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Splash/Splash\|Splash]] is always followed with a number indicating how much [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Splash/Splash\|Splash]] damage is dealt as [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/additional damage\|additional damage]].
