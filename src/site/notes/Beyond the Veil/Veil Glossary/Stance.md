Stance Abilities allow you to take a stance and retain it.  You can exit a stance at any time on your turn as a free action.  You can only use one stance at a time. Unless indicated otherwise a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Stance\|Stance]] lasts until [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/End of Encounter\|End of Encounter]].
