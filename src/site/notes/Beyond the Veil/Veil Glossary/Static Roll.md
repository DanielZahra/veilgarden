A [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Static Roll\|Static Roll]] is used when you make a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Prowess\|Prowess]] roll for an effect that has a duration. The roll’s result is static and persists until the duration of the effect.
