When you [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Strike\|Strike]] with this weapon you [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Attack\|Attack]] in a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Cone/Cone\|Cone]] area. If more than one creature is present within the area, you designate one creature as your [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Attack\|Attack]] target. All effects from abilities or traits only affect the chosen creature. All other creatures in the area take half of the weapon's [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Base Damage\|Base Damage]] on a success; success is calculated on the target of the attack. [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Strike Cone/Strike Cone\|Strike Cone]] is typically followed by a number which indicates the size of the cone.
