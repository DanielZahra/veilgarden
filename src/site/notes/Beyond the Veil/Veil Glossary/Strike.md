You attack with a weapon you're wielding or with an unarmed attack, targeting one creature within your reach (for a melee attack) or within range (for a ranged attack). Roll a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Prowess\|Prowess]] check and compare the result to the target creature's [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Defense\|Defense]] to determine the effect. When making a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Strike\|Strike]] your attack gains the traits for each damage type done.

---
**Critical Success**: You deal double weapon [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Base Damage\|Base Damage]].
**Success**: You deal normal weapon [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Base Damage\|Base Damage]].
**Failure**: You deal no damage

---
Some abilities may call for a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Power Strike\|Power Strike]], this special [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Strike\|Strike]] has the below effects.

**Critical Success:** You deal double weapon [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Base Damage\|Base Damage]].
**Success:** You deal normal weapon [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Base Damage\|Base Damage]].
**Failure:** You deal half **total** damage 
**Critical Failure:** You deal no damage

### Non Lethal Attacks
You can make a [Non-lethal](https://app.notion.com/p/Non-lethal-23b75a22781a809a8c18dabc4194a4de?pvs=21) [Strike](https://app.notion.com/p/Strike-26675a22781a8067882bcfe6823f5f59?pvs=21) even without a [Non-lethal](https://app.notion.com/p/Non-lethal-23b75a22781a809a8c18dabc4194a4de?pvs=21) trait weapon. You simply reduce the degree of success by one.
