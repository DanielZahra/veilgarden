Abilities with the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Summon\|Summon]] trait create magical creatures that resemble real ones but are magical in nature and come into existence for a short period. When summoned, the Summoned creature takes its [[Arrive\|Arrive]] action when you finish using the ability, at the end of your next turn, the summoned creature can either [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Universal  Actions/Step\|Step]] or [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Universal  Actions/Move\|Move]] or take the action for another movement type it has (such as Climb or Burrow), and then takes its [[Depart\|Depart]] action. Then the ability ends. The names of specific Arrive and Depart actions are listed in italics after the word “Arrive” or “Depart” respectively, along with any traits. A creature summoned by you acts in your interests. The summon cannot take any other [[Universal Actions\|Universal Actions]], nor can it be targeted or harmed by Strikes, abilities, or other effects unless they would be able to target or end a ability effect. It has a size for the purposes of determining its placement for effects, but it doesn’t block movement. If applicable, its effects use your own attributes.
