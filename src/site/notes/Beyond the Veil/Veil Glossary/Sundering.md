> When you hit your opponent with [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Sundering\|Sundering]] their defenses are crushed and they are knocked off balance for a time.

- When you successfully hit a target with a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Sundering\|Sundering]] weapon, the target gains the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Exposed\|Exposed]] condition until the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/End of this turn\|End of this turn]].
- Attacks from this weapon ignore  [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Actions/Guard\|Guard]] effects.
