This trait means that the ‘creature’ is made up a number of the same species. A [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Swarm\|Swarm]] is immune to some individual effects but has a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Bane\|Bane]] to [[Area\|Area]] damage.
