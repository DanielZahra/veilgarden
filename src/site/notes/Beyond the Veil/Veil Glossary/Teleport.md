Features or abilities with the Teleport tag allow creatures or objects to teleport immediately to a destination without [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Movement\|Movement]]. You can only [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Teleport\|Teleport]] to unoccupied spaces. For the purposes of [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Held\|Held]] and [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Restrained\|Restrained]], [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Teleport\|Teleport]] usually moves you to a new location where you are no longer suffering from these conditions. ([[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Director\|Director]]‘s decision).
