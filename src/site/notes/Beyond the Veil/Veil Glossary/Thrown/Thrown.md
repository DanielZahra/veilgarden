You can throw this weapon as a ranged attack; it is a ranged weapon when thrown. When this trait appears on a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Melee\|Melee]] weapon You can throw from further than your thrown range but you incur a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Range increment penalty\|Range increment penalty]]. [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Thrown/Thrown\|Thrown]] weapons with the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Melee\|Melee]] trait allow weapon abilities to be used at range. The [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Thrown/Thrown\|Thrown]] trait is followed by a number which denotes its throwing range.
