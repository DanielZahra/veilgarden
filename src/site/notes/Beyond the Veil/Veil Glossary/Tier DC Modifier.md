Tier DC Modifier is used for specificchecks or rituals that increase or decrease in based on the party’s rank against the target. When you need to make a check against a DC and [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Tier DC Modifier\|Tier DC Modifier]] is mentioned, you add or subtract the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Tier DC Modifier\|Tier DC Modifier]] to the check roll. 
e.g.. If an Adept party is making a skill check which is a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Tier DC Modifier\|Tier DC Modifier]] + DC10, and their target is a Veteran Entity. the Final DC is 14. Every tier different between the party and the entity adds or subtracts 4 to the DC.

Examples:

- Party is Adept and the Entity is Veteran: +4 
- Party is Master and the Entity is Veteran: -4
- Party is Adept and Entity is Master: +8
- Party as Veteran and Entity is Veteran: No Change.
