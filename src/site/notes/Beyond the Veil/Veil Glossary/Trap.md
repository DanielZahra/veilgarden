When the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Trap\|Trap]] trait is used, an ability, or item generally requires a condition to be met for the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Trap\|Trap]] to trigger. A [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Trap\|Trap]] does not trigger immediately if deployed in an occupied space. Traps are designed to be inconspicuous. When a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Trap\|Trap]] is deployed, is it considered [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Conditions/Hidden\|Hidden]]. A creature can use a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Actions/Spot\|Spot]] against the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Prowess\|Prowess]] of the deployed [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Trap\|Trap]] to spot the trap you have deployed.
Unless indicated otherwise a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Trap\|Trap]] remains active until it is triggered or until you [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Rest/Rest\|Rest]].
