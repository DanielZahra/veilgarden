Two Handed weapons require two hands to use, but they can be held in 1 hand, freeing up one hand. While [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Two Handed\|Two Handed]] weapons are held in one can, they cannot be used to [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Strike\|Strike]] or trigger abilities. You can use an [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Content/Actions/Interact\|Interact]] with a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Two Handed\|Two Handed]] weapon held in one hand to grip it with two hands.
