> You can use the momentum from a missed attack with this weapon to lead into your next attack. 

When your [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Strike\|Strike]] is a failure, you gain a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Major Boon\|Major Boon]] to [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Prowess\|Prowess]] and 1 [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/additional damage\|additional damage]] for your next [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Strike\|Strike]] during this turn. This [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Boon\|Boon]] can be applied to any [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Strike\|Strike]] for other weapons.
