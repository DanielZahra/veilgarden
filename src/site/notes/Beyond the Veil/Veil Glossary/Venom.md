> Venoms are typically malicious substances that cause a creature to suffer from venom, poison or toxic effects. Venoms are typically delivered through an attacks or ingestion.

To use a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Venom\|Venom]] you make an [[Notion/Universal Actions, Mastery Actions and Activities/Universal  Actions/Interact\|Interact]] to apply the venom to an item, weapon or unarmed. The [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Venom\|Venom]] lasts for  two [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Strike\|Strike]]s or until the effect is applied, whichever happens first. The success is based on your [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Prowess\|Prowess]] made with the attack, this is compared to the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Fortitude\|Fortitude]], [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Reflex\|Reflex]] or [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Will\|Will]] of the creature as an [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Overpower/Overpower\|Overpower]]. You can have a single applied [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Venom\|Venom]] at a time.

---

### Ingested Venom

When a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Venom\|Venom]] is ingested by a creature, you roll [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Prowess\|Prowess]] as if you had a [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Major Boon\|Major Boon]] applied.
