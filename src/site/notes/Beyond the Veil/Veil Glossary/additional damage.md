When a feature or ability mentions [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/additional damage\|additional damage]], unless the damage is specified, it is the same damage as the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/Base Damage\|Base Damage]] of the ability or weapon used.
