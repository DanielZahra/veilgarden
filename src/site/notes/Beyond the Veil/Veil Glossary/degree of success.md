The [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/degree of success\|degree of success]] of a roll, is it’s result compared to a check goal, or dc. It can be critical failure, failure, success or critical success. When an effects increases or decreases the [[Beyond the Veil/Veil Glossary/degree of success\|degree of success]] by 1, the bar simply moves between the 4 degrees.
